Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you book a tour, contact us, or subscribe to our newsletter. This may include your name, email address, phone number, emergency contact information, dietary requirements, and payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, process bookings, communicate with you about your tours, and ensure your safety during mountain activities.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy or as required by law.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You may also opt out of receiving marketing communications from us at any time.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@tatratours.sk.
