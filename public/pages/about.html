About Tatra Tours

Your trusted partner for authentic High Tatras experiences since 2015. Discover our story, meet our team, and learn what makes us Slovakia's premier mountain adventure company.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate mountain enthusiasts and local Slovak guides, Tatra Tours was born from a simple vision: to share the breathtaking beauty and rich cultural heritage of the High Tatras with travelers from around the world.

What started as a small group of friends organizing weekend hiking trips has grown into Slovakia's most trusted mountain tour operator. We've guided over 10,000 guests from 45 countries, creating unforgettable memories while maintaining our commitment to small-group, personalized experiences.

Our deep roots in the region mean we know every trail, every viewpoint, and every local secret. But more importantly, we understand how to create meaningful connections between our guests and this magnificent landscape.
