Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in tour prices?

All our tours include professional mountain guides, accommodation, most meals (breakfast and dinner), transportation during the tour, safety equipment, and comprehensive insurance. Lunch and personal expenses are typically not included.

What fitness level is required?

Our tours cater to different fitness levels. Winter walking holidays are suitable for beginners, while hut-to-hut treks require good physical condition. Each tour page includes detailed difficulty information.

What should I bring?

We provide detailed packing lists for each tour. Generally, you'll need appropriate outdoor clothing, hiking boots, and personal items. We provide technical equipment like snow shoes and safety gear.

Can I book for just one person?

Absolutely! We welcome solo travelers and can often arrange shared accommodation. Single supplements may apply for private rooms.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 30 days before departure receive full refund. Different terms apply for cancellations within 30 days. Travel insurance is recommended.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 8 or more people receive special pricing. Contact us for a custom quote and potential additional discounts for larger groups.
