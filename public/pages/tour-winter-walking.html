Discover the magical winter wonderland of Slovakia's High Tatras on this carefully crafted 5-day walking holiday. Experience pristine snow-covered landscapes, frozen alpine lakes, and the serene beauty of Central Europe's highest mountain range.

Perfect for those seeking a gentle introduction to winter mountain activities, this tour combines guided snow walks with comfortable accommodations and authentic Slovak hospitality. Our experienced local guides will lead you through winter forests, to scenic viewpoints, and share fascinating insights about the region's natural and cultural heritage.
